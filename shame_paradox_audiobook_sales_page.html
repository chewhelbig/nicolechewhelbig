The Shame Paradox

Why we fear most the emotion that keeps us human

Dr Nicole Chew-Helbig, PhD

▶ Narrated by the author ■ Chapter by chapter ■ Includes reflection notes
"The emotion we fear most is the one that makes all of this possible."

Through neuroscience, a reinterpretation of Genesis, and three clinical stories, this book traces shame from infant neurobiology to its place in consciousness itself — arguing that the emotion we spend our lives fleeing is the one that makes us irreplaceably human.
